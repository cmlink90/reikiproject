About

Intuitive sessions by Lily was born out of the desire to bring a deeper meaning to what being an empathic-intuitive really means. The word psychic brings such stigma. However, most people are comfortable with the term intuition. My goal is to assist clients tap into their own inner voice and wisdom. The world is changing. There are plenty of talking heads. Intuitive Sessions by Lily is designed to work collaboratively with individuals: to embrace and to witness growth , while on the journey to learn, to find and to listen to one’s own “psychicness”. We are all blessed with gifts beyond measure. My role is to help individuals realize those gifts, embrace them and watch as they become useful in everyday life.

Philosophy

My practice is rooted in Ayurvedic energy practices. For many years, I believed my practice was to follow Chinese medicine with the additional element of metal. However, I realized after many years, that my soul, my purpose, my practice, rests easy and resonates within the four elements of Ayurvedic philosophy.
